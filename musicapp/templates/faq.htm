Frequently Asked Questions

What is a rendition?

A rendition is a version of a song. Think of it like having various casette tapes of varying qualities.
There may be various casette tapes, one that sounds really good and one that sounds crunchy. The intent of this is to keep stuff sorted.
Currently, renditions have to manually be chosen. In the future, these will automatically be chosen by the highest rated rendition.

What is ratings?

Album and track ratings are for personal "I like this!".
Rendition ratings on the otherhand are used for quality selection.

Are there rules?

  1. Don't add troll songs (EG: Say it is John Music - Song when it is really ear rape)
  2. Don't add invalid metadata.
More will be added as needed. Right now those seem good enough.

What are the user roles?

  1. Banned - Only used in severe cases.
  2. Member - Default role everyone is in.
  3. Trusted - Has access to editing all albums/artists/tracks/renditions, including locked ones.
  4. Moderator - Same as trusted but can appoint trusted role, but can also delete entries and ban people.
  5. Admin - Backend access, can appoint moderators.

How does artwork work?

Please use 256x256 textures.
